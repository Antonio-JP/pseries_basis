Factorial Basis: simplest Polynomial basis¶

Subpackage for Factorial basis.

A factorial basis is a basis of the ring of formal power series \(\mathbb{K}[[x]]\) where the elements of the basis are polynomials of increasing degree such that:

\[P_{n+1}(x) = (a_n*x + b_n)P_n(x)\]

There are plenty of types and extensions of Factorial basis. This subpackage will include all of them, such as ProductBasis or SievedBasis.
